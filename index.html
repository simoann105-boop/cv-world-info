Privacy Policy for CV WORLD

Effective Date: April 6, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how CV WORLD collects, uses, and protects your information.

1. Information We Collect

To provide our services, we collect the following:

  • Personal Information: Name and Email address provided during registration.
  • Device Security: We collect a Secure Device Fingerprint to prevent fraud and ensure fair use of our referral system.

2. Purpose of Data

We use this data to deliver professional ATS-ready CV templates and provide live job alerts for Doha and throughout Qatar.

3. Account and Data Deletion

We respect your privacy and provide you with the ability to delete your account and all associated data permanently.

How to request deletion: To delete your account, please send an email to [ضع إيميلك هنا] with the subject line "Request for Account Deletion". Our team will process your request promptly.

What is deleted: Upon your request, we will permanently delete your Name, Email address, saved CV data, and your referral progress.

Data Retention: Some technical logs may be kept for up to 30 days for security purposes before being permanently wiped.

4. Third-Party Services

We utilize trusted services to maintain app functionality:

  • Firebase: For secure user authentication and database management.
  • OneSignal: To notify you of new job postings in Qatar and app updates.

5. Contact

For any privacy concerns, contact us through the CV WORLD app support or via email at the address provided in Section 3.
